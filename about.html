About me

Hello! I am Uladzislau, you can call me Ulad. Glad to see you in my portfolio! Now I will tell you a little about myself.

I am a junior full stack developer, my tech stack is: Python/Django, JavaScript/Vue, HTML, CSS, MySQL

On March 26, 2021, I decided to start learning programming. I had little idea about programming, but since childhood I have been interested in computers and IT technologies, as a result of which programming has always attracted me

I started my programming journey by learning Python. This is a very pleasant language for beginners, it helped me understand what programming is all about.

I started with free courses and books, as well as educational videos on YouTube. From the very beginning, I studied the development of various projects, from game development using PyGame library, to solving mathematical problems in some courses.

After learning the basics, I chose my path of becoming in programming and chose web development. Immediately after, I continued to study Python, but already on the backend side using the Django framework.

From now on, I will only focus on back-end web development with Django. I studied and tried to create something. At first it was very unclear how it works and how to write it to make it work. But, over time, I began to notice that I was becoming more and more competent. I started my training with at least an hour a day, at the very beginning I got 3 hours a day, but not always, then I began to increase this value, from one hour a day to three, and after - up to 5-6 hours a day.

Over time, I fell in love with web development and programming in general, I was very interested in learning something new in this direction, as a result of which I realized that I want to connect my life with programming forever.

With the passage of time, I realized that development on the server side alone was not enough for me, I wanted to make the functionality I made look beautiful, so I began to delve into HTML, CSS and JavaScript.

After 564 days of training, I am confident in my abilities and ready to get my first job as a web developer, ready to create and develop my skills in this direction, and also ready to make life easier for other people, thanks to the products in the development of which I will participate.
